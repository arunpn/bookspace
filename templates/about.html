About

Bookspace is a tool for exploring a similarity space of books. To search for similar books, enter a book title (chosen from the autocomplete suggestions), or enter one or more positive words, or both.

The similarity space was learned using a neural network approach called doc2vec (or paragraph vector ). Given a corpus of documents, doc2vec learns a vector space representation of documents and words, such that similar documents and words are given nearby vectors. The bookspace model was trained on user reviews by concatenating each book's reviews into a single "document". By representing each book as a document, doc2vec learns an item similarity space.
